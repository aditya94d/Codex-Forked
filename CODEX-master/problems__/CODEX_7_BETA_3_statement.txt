Given N.Calculate number of tuples (i,j) for all i\>=1 
and j\>=1, such that i*j\<N. <br/><br/>
PS. (1,2) and (2,1) are different. <br/><br/>

<h3>Input Format:</h3><br/>
First Line Number Of test case(t), followed by t numbers(N).<br/>
2\<=N\<=10^10 <br/><br/>
<h3>Output Format:</h3><br/>Print ans for each N.<br/><br/>

<h3>Sample:</h3><br/>
<h4>Input:</h4><br/>
2<br/>
3<br/>
4<br/><br/>

<h4>Output:</h4><br/>
3<br/>
5<br/><br/>

<h3>Explanation:</h3><br/>
For N=3 pairs are (1,1),(1,2),(2,1)<br/><br/>
